Will the Seattle Mariners make the playoffs?

The idea here is to guess the amount of wins the Seattle Mariners will end up with after their 162-game season

How to Play

  1. Guess how many games the M's will win this year.
  2. Try your hardest to guess more than 70 games. If you don't, you'll be sorry.
  3. Keep guessing to find out if the Mariners should make the playoffs.
